Crop Water Requirements

Paddy

Water Level: 2-3 inches

Frequency: Maintain standing water during the growing period.

Additional Info: Ensure proper drainage during the maturation stage to avoid waterlogging.

Cotton

Water Level: 1.5-2.5 inches

Frequency: Every 15 days in dry conditions.

Additional Info: Critical stages for irrigation include flowering and boll formation.

Banana

Water Level: 4-6 inches

Frequency: Weekly during dry periods or as needed to maintain soil moisture.

Additional Info: Avoid water stagnation around the roots to prevent diseases.
